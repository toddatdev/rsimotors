Membership Terms & Conditions

How does the RSI Buyers Club Work?

Once you sign up or click the level that best fits your needs, you will be taken to a sign-up site where you will enter all your info in. Once you submit that, you will be brought back to our site where you will create a login into your account for the RSI Buyers Club. Under your Account Page, you will be asked to include which vehicle/vehicles you currently have in your garage, there will be spots for up to 3 vehicles that you can list. The reason we ask this, is so we do not have one account buying parts for a group of friends, if they wish to sign up, they are welcome to and its why we offer the different membership levels too. There will be rewards for referring a new member to us and you can even earn up to 12 free months by referring your friends and family (coming soon). To get access to your wholesale pricing, you must log in to your account every time you visit the site, otherwise, you will be shown the regular online pricing.

Why are there different pricing levels?

We understand that there are projects small and big and budgets that can vary for their vehicles. We want and feel, it is important to find a way to offer this program to Everyone! This should not just be for the wealthy and large builds but for the beginner as well. Anyone that has the passion and drive to modify their vehicle should have access to this incredible way to buy parts.

Why is there a 12-month commitment?

Again, we do not want people to take advantage of the program. When we offer our true cost plus a few percent (to cover credit card fees), that is truly what we are offering our members, so the membership dues are how we stay alive and strong to continue the program. The membership fee is what keeps us alive and allows us to bring you this incredible program. If we have people continuously canceling after one month and ordering all their parts, we would not be able to continue to offer the program. If someone does want to cancel after one month and has already ordered parts, you will be charged for the remaining months you have not used. If you cancel after one month, you will be charged for 11 more months, if you cancel after 6 months you will be charged 6 more months, etc. If you do not end up ordering any parts, then we will cancel and not charge you anything else.

Why is there a spending cap for the membership levels?

We must do this so people do not take advantage of the program. There will be a level that will not have a spending cap but that person will pay more to get that unlimited spend. We understand that everyone has a budget for their vehicle whether it is a truck, car, SUV, or ATV and some people will only spend a few hundred bucks on parts per year which is why we have a program for every budget. Everyone should have access to the same pricing!

What is the RSI Buyers Club all about?

This is a unique concept and a new way for automotive enthusiasts, restorers, cruisers and weekend racers to purchase performance parts and accessories at significant discounts! By becoming an RSI Buyers Club member and paying a small monthly fee, you will get access to our true wholesale pricing. Have you heard of the "big box" wholesale membership clubs for groceries and housewares? Well, we're taking that same concept and applying it to automotive customization. For the price of a couple trips to the local coffee shop or ordering takeout one night a month, you can have access to our wholesale pricing and shipping discounts. With warehouses spread out across the US, most parts are delivered within 2-3 business days if they are in stock. We're here to help with everything from outfitting your dream build to restoring that project car you just couldn't pass up - all while saving you money along the way!

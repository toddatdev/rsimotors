Order and Shipment Tracking

Upon your purchase, you will recieve a reciept via email with your order and tracking information once the order has been processed.

You can view your order by logging into your account. Once you have signed into your account, click "My account". A small drop down list will show, click the "My account" option again. This will take you to all of your account information. Next click the "Orders" tab and your order history will be displayed along with any other order information (Order No, Order Date, Order Status, etc)


In order to check the status and location of your package, you will need to visit the shipping company's website below and enter your tracking number.
